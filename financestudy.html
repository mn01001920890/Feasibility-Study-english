Executive Statement

This study aims to evaluate the financial feasibility of establishing a project specializing in car protection services using nano-ceramic technology and PPF films in Riyadh, specifically in the area between the Al-Munsiyah and Qurtubah neighborhoods. This project responds to the growing Saudi automotive market and the increasing demand for advanced car care services.

The project requires an initial investment of 114,148 SAR in fixed assets (including establishment expenses), with a total paid-up capital of 250,000 SAR. Financial projections indicate sustainable revenue growth from 1.1 million SAR in the first year to 1.56 million SAR in the fourth year, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.3%.

The updated financial results show that the project achieves excellent economic performance, with a Net Present Value (NPV) of 130,241 SAR at a 12% discount rate, an Internal Rate of Return (IRR) of 30%, and a short payback period of 2.56 years. These indicators confirm the strength of the business model and its ability to generate rewarding returns for investors.

The project is managed by two equal partners in capital: Eng. Mahmoud Mostafa and Dr. Hassan Mahni (50% each). Both partners have extensive experience in their respective fields and a strong local network, which enhances the project's chances of success and future growth.

Project Description & Financial Assumptions

Introduction and Project Description

1.1 Market Overview

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is witnessing sustained growth in the automotive sector, driven by Vision 2030 and rapid economic development. According to the latest statistics, the Saudi market has more than 12 million registered vehicles, with an annual growth rate ranging between 5% and 8%. Riyadh, as the capital and main economic center, accounts for about 25% of the total vehicles in the Kingdom.

The Saudi market is characterized by high-income levels and increasing awareness of the importance of car care and preserving its value. This trend has created a growing demand for advanced protection services such as nano-ceramics and PPF films, which are considered a long-term investment to maintain the car's exterior appearance and investment value.

1.2 Strategic Location

The area between the Al-Munsiyah and Qurtubah neighborhoods in Riyadh was chosen based on a careful study of demographic and economic factors. This area is characterized by a high population density of middle and upper classes, with a high ownership rate of luxury and modern cars. The area also enjoys easy access and transportation, making it an ideal location to attract customers from all over Riyadh.

Market studies indicate that the target area has more than 150,000 vehicles, with an annual renewal rate of 15%, providing a large and renewable customer base for the project.

1.3 Nature of Services Provided

The project offers a comprehensive range of advanced car protection services, including:

Nano-Ceramic Services

An advanced technology that provides a transparent and durable protective layer that preserves the car's paint from weather factors and minor scratches. It is available in three levels: two layers, three layers, and four layers, to meet the needs of different customers and their budgets.

Paint Protection Films (PPF)

Advanced protection technology using transparent polyurethane films that provide superior protection against scratches, gravel, and weather effects. Available for partial protection (front, rear, doors) or full car protection.

Complementary Services

Includes thermal tinting, washing and polishing, interior cleaning and sanitizing, and minor scratch repair, providing comprehensive car care solutions.

Integrated Packages

The Full Protection Package and the luxurious VIP Package, which combine several services at competitive prices to provide greater value to customers.

1.4 Target Audience

The project targets several customer segments:

Owners of luxury and modern cars: who seek to maintain the value of their investments and ensure the elegant appearance of their cars.
Companies and institutions: that own fleets of cars and need regular maintenance and protection services.
Car enthusiasts: who pay special attention to details and seek the highest quality in car care services.
