A comprehensive overview of the marketing study results

Study Objective

This marketing study aims to analyze the market and identify suitable marketing strategies for the Luxury Car project, which specializes in car protection and nano-ceramic services. The project is strategically located between Al-Munsiyah and Qurtubah, aiming to provide advanced car protection services in an area characterized by a high density of mid-range and luxury car owners.
