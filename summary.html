Vision

To become the number one reference in the field of car protection and nano-ceramic on both the local and global levels by building an interconnected brand based on innovation, quality, and total customer satisfaction.

Mission

To provide integrated and professional car protection services using the latest nano and protective film technologies, delivered by well-trained teams, and offering a seamless digital experience from booking to delivery. We are committed to offering our services at competitive prices to ensure customer satisfaction.

Objectives & Goals

  • Reach luxury car owners in Riyadh through high-value services.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction with a smooth experience, including transport, authorization, and after-sales service.
  • Build strong brand loyalty through uncompromised quality.
  • Achieve steady annual growth in customers and revenue.

Long-Term Goals

  • Open a second branch within the first year, then expand to several major areas in Riyadh.
  • Build a branch network covering all regions of the Kingdom by the fourth year.
  • Launch operations in the Gulf and global markets at a later stage.
  • Achieve international recognition for the Luxury Car brand as a standard of quality and excellence.

Growth Plan

  • Enhance digital presence through a smart booking platform and comprehensive electronic authorization for clients.
  • Launch mobile units equipped with all technologies to serve customers at their locations.
  • Provide a transportation fleet for car pickup and delivery, ensuring a seamless and comfortable experience.
  • Establish strategic partnerships with car agencies and major showrooms from the outset.
  • Continual investment in modern technologies and training to maintain leadership.

Competitive Advantage

  • Superior Quality: Using the best materials and modern technologies for car protection, ensuring perfect results and long-lasting shine.
  • Expertise & Professionalism: A specialized and well-trained team with vast experience, providing service at the highest levels of professionalism and attention to detail.
  • Outstanding Customer Experience: Providing an integrated and smooth customer journey, including online booking, transportation & authorization, and ongoing support to ensure their comfort.
  • Competitive Pricing: Offering our luxury services at competitive prices with high value for clients, guaranteeing their full satisfaction.
  • Continuous Innovation: Our commitment to ongoing innovation and development keeps us at the forefront of the car protection field, delivering the latest solutions to our clients.
