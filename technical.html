Specialized Technical Operations

Detailed application procedures for all services

Nano Ceramic Application Procedures

Phase 1: Preparation and Setup (60-90 minutes)
Thorough inspection of the car's paint condition using a paint thickness gauge. Deep cleaning with high-pressure water and specialized shampoo. Decontamination using a specialized clay bar. Complete drying with high-absorbency microfiber towels.
Phase 2: Treatment and Final Preparation (45-60 minutes)
Application of a specialized degreasing cleaner. Use of a surface activator to prepare the paint. Final inspection with a digital microscope. Ensuring the temperature (20-25¬∞C) and humidity (<60%) are optimal.
Phase 3: Nano Ceramic Application (120-180 minutes)
Dividing the car into small sections (60x60 cm). Distributing the material in cross-hatch motions to ensure even coverage. Allowing the material to bond with the paint. Polishing and finishing with clean microfiber towels.
Phase 4: Final Curing and Inspection (30-45 minutes)
Leaving the car in a controlled environment for 24 hours. Water drop test to confirm hydrophobic properties. Measuring gloss and contact angle. Documenting the results with high-resolution photos.

PPF Protection Film Application Procedures

Preparation and Measurement (90-120 minutes)
Thorough inspection of the car's paint and identifying sensitive areas. Specialized cleaning with high-pressure water. Accurate measurement of the areas to be covered. Drawing a detailed diagram for the required film cuts.
Cutting and Preparing the Film (60-90 minutes)
Cutting the film 2-3 cm larger than required. Preparing the application solution (distilled water + isopropyl alcohol). Numbering and organizing the pieces according to the application order.
Gradual Application (180-300 minutes)
Spraying the surface heavily with the application solution. Carefully placing the film and positioning it correctly. Removing air and liquid with specialized tools. Shaping the film around curves using controlled heat.

Tinting and Thermal Insulation Procedures

Glass Inspection and Cleaning (45-60 minutes)
Thorough inspection of the car's glass condition and identifying any defects. Cleaning the glass inside and out with a specialized cleaner. Measuring the dimensions of each glass with millimeter precision.
Specialized Application (90-150 minutes)
Spraying the glass with the application solution. Placing the film very carefully to avoid bubbles. Removing air and water with specialized tools. Cutting the excess film with high precision.

Interior and Exterior Polishing Procedures

Advanced Exterior Polishing
Thorough washing and decontamination. Applying polishing compound in small circular motions. Using a rotary machine at an appropriate speed. Applying the final polish and buffing with clean towels.
Specialized Interior Polishing
Cleaning leather seats with a specialized cleaner. Treating plastic surfaces with an anti-UV polish. Cleaning and deodorizing the A/C with a specialized cleaner.
